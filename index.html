About US

Our aim is to teach you about cyber security. Visitors can learn about cyber security by playing our "Spammy" game.

Spammy Game

Spammy is a game about Spammy, a cute fox, who likes adventure. Spammy is the symbol of Firefox and other web browsers that we use and we can get attacked online through those web browsers. As Spammy travels, he can meet traps. If Spammy falls into trap, he needs to learn about cyber security to get out of the trap. Spammy can also earn rewards by eating food.
